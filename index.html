Starting a fitness journey can be confusing, but you don't need a gym membership to get fit. Here is a simple, no-equipment routine designed specifically for beginners to do right in their living room.

The 3-Step Beginner Home Workout Plan

  1. Bodyweight Squats (10 reps): Great for building leg strength. Keep your chest up and lower your hips as if sitting back into an invisible chair.
  2. Incline Push-Ups (8 reps): Place your hands on a sturdy wall, bed, or table. This is easier than floor push-ups and perfect for beginners to build upper body strength.
  3. Jumping Jacks (30 seconds): A brilliant way to get your heart rate up, burn calories, and build basic cardio endurance.
